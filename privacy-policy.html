Findi Play Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

Findi Play is a game-discovery app. This policy explains what information the app handles. The short version: Findi Play does not sell or share your personal data. An account is optional — without one, nothing you do leaves your device.

Information stored on your device

Your platform and subscription selections, your favourite genres, and your backlog/wishlist are stored on your device. If you do not create an account, this information never leaves your device and is deleted when you delete the app.

Accounts (optional)

You can use Findi Play entirely without an account. If you choose to create one, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password in order to identify you and sync your data. We never see your password in readable form.

With an account, your platform/subscription selections, favourite genres and backlog are stored on our servers so they sync across your devices. That is the only information synced. Accounts and authentication are operated for us by Supabase.

Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account at any time from inside the app: Settings → Account → Delete account. This immediately and irreversibly deletes your account and all synced data from our servers.

Information sent over the network

To show game information, the app requests data from game-metadata services (RAWG, and IGDB via our own server). Like any internet request, these carry your IP address, which is used transiently to return the response and is not used by us to identify you.

When you use the AI Finder, the text of your search (for example, “something cozy I can finish in a weekend”) is sent to our server and processed by an AI provider to generate recommendations. Searches are not linked to your identity, and we do not store them. AI providers process this text under their own data-processing terms.

What we don't do

  • No analytics or advertising SDKs, and no tracking across apps
  • No sale or sharing of personal information
  • No collection of names, contacts, location, or payment details
  • No data collection from children; the app has no social features

Third-party data sources

Game titles, artwork, and metadata are provided by RAWG and IGDB under their respective terms.
