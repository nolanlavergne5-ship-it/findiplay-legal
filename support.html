Common questions

How do I change my platforms or subscriptions?
Tap the settings icon (top right of Discover) and toggle any platform or service on or off.

Why don't I see subscription catalogs yet?
Live Game Pass / PS Plus / EA Play / Apple Arcade / Play Pass catalog tracking is in development. Your selections are saved and will light up when it ships.

How does the AI Finder work?
Describe a mood, a time budget, or a game you half-remember. Findi Play matches your description against game data and recommends titles you can actually play on your platforms.

Where does the game data come from?
Game metadata and artwork come from RAWG and IGDB.
